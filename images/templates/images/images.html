How to Use the Image Upload App

This application allows you to view or upload images for specific part numbers. Follow these easy steps:

  • Step 1: In the browser's address bar, type the part number at the end of the URL and press Enter. Example: http://yourdomain.com/images/1234
  • Step 2: If an image is found for the part number, it will be displayed immediately.
  • Step 3: If no image is found, you'll have the option to upload one directly from your device.
  • Step 4: Once uploaded, the image will be displayed automatically.

Start by entering a part number in the URL!
